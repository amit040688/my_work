Our Services

In the early days, we were a scrappy startup determined to stand out. We knew we couldn't compete with the IT giants on size, so we focused on agility and exceptional customer service. We recruited brilliant minds who were obsessed with delighting our clients. This led to the creation of UPSALA Support®, our end-to-end customer service model. We honed our technical expertise, becoming fluent in the languages of front-end and back-end development, wielding 256 encryption databases like second nature. We mastered the intricacies of Linux, Windows, and network security, all in pursuit of crafting the industry's most robust managed hosting service. Client recommendations fueled our growth, propelling us to the forefront of the industry. Fast forward to 2014, the cloud revolution arrived. Businesses gained access to vast, on-demand computing resources. But to leverage this new power, they needed cloud infrastructure gurus – individuals who could navigate the complex landscape of tools, applications, and databases that resided within. Today, UPSALA DEFSOL AND IT SERVICES PRIVATE LIMITED stands as a leading innovator, providing cutting-edge expertise and management solutions in Artificial Intelligence and Drone Technology across the entire defense sector. We are equally adept at managing public and private cloud technologies. Our team of passionate experts blends proactive, 24/7 service with best-in-class automation tools, ensuring you receive the technology you need, exactly when you need it.
